# Metric Definitions

This document summarises the main business metrics used across the project.

## Financial Metrics

### Net Profit
Revenue remaining after subtracting product cost and allocated shipping cost.

### Net Margin %
Net Profit divided by Revenue.

### Revenue At Risk
Revenue linked to customers with complaint activity who have not yet churned.

### Revenue Lost
Revenue linked to customers with complaint activity who are already classified as churned.

---

## Customer Metrics

### Customer Share
The proportion of total customers represented by a segment.

### Profit Share
The proportion of total net profit represented by a segment.

### Churn Probability
A modelled probability score generated in Python to prioritise customer retention risk.

### High-Value At-Risk Customers
Customers with both elevated churn probability and strong commercial value.

---

## Service Metrics

### Avg CSAT Score
Average customer-level service satisfaction score, derived from complaint-linked ticket data.

### Total Tickets Count
The number of support tickets associated with a customer.

### CSAT Risk Band
A service-quality grouping used in Dashboard 2 to show how customer experience quality links to churn risk.

---

## Behaviour Metrics

### Avg Basket Size
Average number of order lines per customer order.

### Market Basket Support
The proportion of orders containing a given product pair.

### Market Basket Confidence
The likelihood of Product B being purchased when Product A appears.

### Market Basket Lift
The strength of association between two products compared with chance.

### Total Pair Net Profit
The total estimated net profit generated by a product pairing.
